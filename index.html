SCP-002

SCP-002 resembles a tumorous, fleshy growth with a volume of roughly 60 m³ (or 2000 ft³). An iron valve hatch on one side leads to its interior, which appears to be a standard low-rent apartment of modest size.

SCP-003

SCP-003 is a complex entity composed of microscopic, amorphous biological material of unknown origin. It exhibits a strong compulsion effect on those who view it directly, compelling them to assist in its containment and protection.

SCP-004

SCP-004 is a wooden door frame, approximately 80 cm wide and 190 cm tall. Any object or material which passes through the door frame is immediately translocated to an unknown location, never to return.

SCP-005

SCP-005 is a small key of unknown make and metal, appearing to be designed for a pin tumbler lock. It possesses the unique ability to open any lock it is inserted into, regardless of its design or complexity.

SCP-006

SCP-006 is a crystalline mineral substance that rapidly accelerates the healing process of living tissue. When applied to a wound, it causes rapid regeneration, often healing injuries within seconds.
